Quick Troubleshooting

App crashes on launch
Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store and try restarting your device. If the crash continues, include device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce when contacting support.
Cannot sign in
Check your network connection and try resetting the app (sign out, force-quit, reopen). If that fails, send a screenshot and the email you used to register.
Missing content or sync issues
Allow the app a few minutes to sync. If items still do not appear, include the track/album details and your account ID.
